Our Services

  • Comprehensive eye examinations
  • Individualized contact lens fitting and services
  • Diagnosis and treatment of eye diseases, including glaucoma, macular degeneration, diabetes, hypertension, and cataracts.
  • Treatment and managemnt of Dry Eyes Disease, tearing, and eye irritations

  • Eyeglass repairs and adjustments
  • Nutritional counseling for the prevention and treatment of macular degeneration and dry eye disease.
